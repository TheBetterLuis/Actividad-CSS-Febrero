The heartbeat of rhythm and the home of premier e-drum kits since 1990. Our journey began over three decades ago, with a zeal for percussion and a commitment to excellence that has resonated through the music industry. 1990: It all started in a compact workshop where our founders, driven by their love for beats, assembled their first electronic drum kit. These kits quickly became renowned for their outstanding sound quality and resilience. 2015: Celebrating 25 years of innovation, we expanded our facilities to accommodate the increasing demand for our products. We introduced cutting-edge technology and materials, establishing new standards in electronic drum kit production. 2024: Today, we lead the industry in electronic drum kit manufacturing, with a legacy of unmatched craftsmanship. Our drum kits are the preferred choice of drummers worldwide, from emerging artists to celebrated professionals. At DrumsDrumsDrums, we’re not just producing instruments; we’re creating the tools for artists to articulate their rhythm, spirit, and passion. Join us on our perpetual quest for the perfect beat, as we march to the rhythm of innovation and tradition.
